Few words about us

BeFiT was created by just two students: a team of young, soon-to-be software engineers who thought fitness should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their income. Both Filip and Viktor felt like there was a lack of reliable health and fitness information on the web, and too many people in the industry were more focused on monetary gain or appearance than they were on good health.
